Competition

Theme

This year's theme will be space, as we choose different themes each year. Accordingly, we expect space-related games from competitors.

 

 

 

Who can participate

All middle and highschool students can participate in the competition.

 

 

 

 

Rules

Competitor teams develop and present games in accordance with the shared score criteria and them. Competitior teams must develop their games using Blender and Unreal Engine.Teams will be ranked according to the points they have earned.

 

Date & Time

Game Development Days is planed to start on February 20, 2022.The presentations will be held on February 27 and award ceremony will be on the afternoon of the same day.
